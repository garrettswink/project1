About

Arthur Herbert Fonzarelli Institue for Water Skiing and Aquatic Athletics is a unique institution that offers courses on water sports and stunts. It was founded by Fonzie, a legendary mechanic, teacher, and biker who became famous for his daring feat of jumping over a shark on water skis. The academy aims to inspire and train the next generation of aquatic athletes who want to follow in Fonzie's footsteps and challenge themselves to new heights of adventure. Whether you want to learn how to water ski, surf, or scuba dive, the academy has something for you. Join us today and discover the thrill of aquatic athletics!
